Certification Engineer (CE)

Location: Wichita KS

Description: 3S Engineering

This position exercises independent judgment with regard to the resolution of problems and interaction with internal and external departments, outside vendors, subcontractors and consultants, ODA unit members and customers. This position also provides oversight on 3S Certification ODA activities and has direct contact with the FAA.

PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:

• Understand, plan and execute technical activities in accordance with 3S Engineering policies, procedures and work instructions and Customer specific requirements

• Interfaces and communicates with 3S Engineering Program Management, 3S Certification ODA members/staff, all branches of the FAA,  and customers as necessary

• Develop, edit and contribute to reports supporting certification activities such as FAA Project Specific Certification Plans, test plans and test reports, compliance reports, etc.

• Assist the assigned project manager for 3S Certification ODA projects with certification requirements for systems and equipment, structures, power plants and flight test requirements defined by the 3S Certification ODA Unit

• Create necessary supporting documentation for STC Projects required within specific technical disciplines and/or capabilities

• Serve as interface between the 3S Engineering design team, project team, DER, UMs and the 3S Certification ODA administrative staff

• Serve as Subject Matter Expert in order to expedite projects and upgrade the 3S Engineering staff’s knowledge level related to FAA requirements

• Interface with customers to generate Statements of Work, Concept of Operations documents and systems specifications.

• Provide support to internal resources in the generation of internal specifications, control documents, verification and validation plans, procedures, reports or other documentation in support of customer requirements

• Provide technical support and engineering expertise to Business Development activities as required

• Assist senior management in providing opportunities for career advancement and growth for engineering personnel balancing personal aspirations with company objectives

• Attend project meetings and assume responsibility for assigned action items

• Punctuality to work each day and prepared to work scheduled work hours

• Other duties as assigned
